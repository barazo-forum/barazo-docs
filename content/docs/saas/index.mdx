Welcome to the Barazo managed hosting guide.

## Getting Started

Your Barazo forum is fully managed -- no servers to maintain, no Docker to configure. Sign in to your admin dashboard to manage everything.

- **Billing & Subscriptions** -- Plans, invoices, upgrading, downgrading, VAT

- **Usage & Limits** -- What's included in your plan and what happens at limits
- **Managed Backups** -- How backups work and how to request a restore
- **Data Export** -- Export your data or migrate away from SaaS
- **Support** -- How to get help and expected response times
- **Community Settings** -- Moderation, categories, branding, content maturity, onboarding fields
- **FAQ** -- Common questions and troubleshooting
